IIT Madras: Cutoff, Courses, Placement, Fees, Admission, Ranking

College Information

IIT Madras is the best engineering institute in India. The institute has been ranked at 1 by the NIRF for the past 5 years. IIT Madras offers a total of 121 courses including the BTech and Online degree programs. IIT Madras BTech is the flagship program of the institute having an intake capacity of 877 seats. Admission to the program depends on the JEE Advanced exam scores and participation in the JoSAA counseling. IIT Madras Cutoff for the BTech program ranges between 85-8000 in the JEE Advanced exam. Candidates applying for IIT Madras admission are charged an annual fee of INR 2.05 Lacs for the BTech program. Another program at the institute popular amongst the aspirants is the IIT Madras Online Degree. The online degree includes BSc in Programming & Data Science and Diploma in Programming & Data Science. The fees for the program ranges between INR 50k to 1 Lac.

In 2003, IIT Madras launched NPTEL (National Program on Technology Enhanced Learning). In a short period of time, this program grew in popularity among Indian students. The program included all IITs, which introduced a series of videos based on courses from all engineering specializations. It is currently India's largest online repository of lectures. The platform includes videos and lectures on subjects such as engineering, basic sciences, and selected humanities and social sciences.
